3. Kevin Indig 
    - https://www.linkedin.com/posts/kevinindig_operationalising-topic-first-seo-in-5-steps-activity-7350975378233954305-1hpW/
    Content:
    Operationalising Topic-First SEO in 5 Steps:

    1. Audit personas & problems – real interviews > search-volume spreadsheets.
    2. Design a pillar-and-cluster map – but match pages to concepts, not exact phrases.
    3. Invest in zero-volume questions – they build authority and capture long-tail AI answers.
    4. Add information gain – proprietary data, scripts, or unique perspectives that others can quote.
    5. Measure topical breadth & depth – track coverage gaps and entity visibility, not just rank positions.

    Backlinko proved you don’t need 1,000 posts - just comprehensive ones.

    Build topic systems, not articles.
